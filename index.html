About Me

I am a Master’s student in Economics with a focus on applied microeconomics, data science, and machine learning. My work combines causal inference methods with modern ML tools for prediction and text analysis.

I am particularly interested in labor markets, policy evaluation, and behavioral responses to economic incentives. Through academic research and independent projects, I emphasize clean data construction, transparent methodology, and clear communication of results.
