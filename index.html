Featured Research

MA Thesis · 2026
Event study · age groups
Sector-Specific Wage Floors and Young Adult Labor Market Outcomes: Evidence from California's AB 1228
Michael Early · MA Thesis · California State University, Fullerton · 2026
Committee: Maria Casanova (chair), Pedro Amaral, Kristin Kleinjans

CPS + QCEW evidence on the first statewide sector-specific wage floor in US history. All-state TWFE, synthetic control, and contiguous-county border designs. The aggregate youth employment effect is small and not robust; adjustment runs through the intensive margin (wages up 2.1%, hours down 3.8%); and the paper's central contribution is compositional — workers without a high school diploma or with only a high school diploma see ~3.2 pp employment declines while those with some college see ~2.4 pp gains.
