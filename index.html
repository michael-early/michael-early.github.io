About Me

I'm an MA Economics candidate at Cal State Fullerton (GPA 3.93) specializing in quantitative methods, causal inference, and applied data science. My work combines econometric identification strategies like difference-in-differences, regression discontinuity, and instrumental variables with machine learning for prediction, classification, and NLP.

I'm currently a Graduate Intern in Enterprise Architecture at First American, building LLM-powered tools and Power BI dashboards. My research interests are in labor markets, policy evaluation, and market structure, and I care about taking projects from messy raw data all the way to results that are actually useful.
