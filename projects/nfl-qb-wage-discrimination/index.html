Is There Wage Discrimination Among NFL Quarterbacks?

Labor Economics · Sports Economics

Research Question

Do NFL quarterbacks receive different levels of guaranteed compensation on their second contracts based on race, after controlling for performance and market conditions?

Data

  • NFL quarterbacks drafted 2011–2020
  • Guaranteed money on second contracts (Spotrac)
  • Quarterback Rating (QBR)
  • Draft position and team salary cap data

Methodology

  • Ordinary Least Squares regression
  • Race indicator variable
  • Controls for performance, draft capital, and cap space
  • RESET test for functional form validation

Key Findings

  • No statistically significant relationship between race and guaranteed money
  • Quarterback performance (QBR) strongly predicts compensation
  • Team cap space positively affects guaranteed contract values
