Fast-Food Minimum Wage (AB 1228)(Working Paper)

Question: What was the short-run impact of California’s $20 fast-food minimum wage on youth employment?

Data

  • CPS ORG microdata (IPUMS)
  • California vs bordering control states
  • Sample: ages 16–24

Methodology

  • Difference-in-Differences (DiD)
  • Triple Difference (DDD)
  • Robust standard errors

Key Findings

  • No immediate collapse in youth employment
  • Heterogeneous effects by metro area
  • Results consistent with partial adjustment
