Forecasting Super Bowl Odds

Time-Series & Sports Economics

Research Question

Can betting market odds for Super Bowl futures be used to generate accurate forecasts of championship outcomes, and do these markets exhibit efficiency over the NFL season?

Data

  • Historical Super Bowl futures odds
  • Implied probabilities derived from betting lines
  • Weekly observations across NFL seasons

Methodology

  • ARIMA and ARIMAX time-series models
  • Probability calibration and forecast evaluation
  • Comparison of model forecasts vs market-implied probabilities

Key Findings

  • Betting markets rapidly incorporate new information
  • Short-horizon forecasts outperform long-horizon predictions
  • Limited but detectable inefficiencies early in the season
